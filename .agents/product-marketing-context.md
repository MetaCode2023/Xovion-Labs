## Target Audience

**Primary customers:**
- **Operators** running service businesses, real estate operations, or small portfolios of companies — people who need AI to actually take action inside their software, not give them generic advice
- **Small-to-mid businesses** whose website is a disconnected brochure that doesn't talk to their pipeline
- **Sales teams** drowning in messy CRMs where leads sit in a generic "Follow Up" bucket
- **Service businesses** (home services, contractors, real estate, property management) where missed calls = missed jobs

**Geography**: Sioux Falls SD home base, but the work is delivered remotely — service area is effectively national.

**Who is NOT the customer:**
- Enterprise buyers looking for white-glove SaaS sold by a sales team
- Anyone wanting a slide deck and a "framework" without execution
- Companies looking to hire a full-time engineering team
- People shopping primarily on price (we're not the cheapest)

- Role: Owner/founder of a service business or small portfolio (real estate, contractor, home services), $1M–$20M revenue
- Situation: Running ops himself or with a small team; has tried 3+ AI tools and ChatGPT subs that didn't stick

- Situation: CRM is messy. Reps don't know who to call today. Hot leads slip; cold leads get over-nurtured.
- Goals: A pipeline that tells the team exactly what to do next, automation that catches stalled deals, real intelligence on what's actually working

## Common Objections

- **"We already use ChatGPT"** → ChatGPT is a smart search engine. We give AI hands inside your actual software — booking appointments, updating CRMs, surfacing stalled deals. That's a different category.
- **"AI agencies have burned us before"** → We're not an agency selling something we don't use. Every system we build is running in our own businesses first. That's why we recommend it.
- **"It's too expensive vs. SaaS"** → SaaS is rented. We help you stop renting and own infrastructure built for your operation. Lower cost over 12 months and better fit.
- **"We don't have time for a 3-month implementation"** → Most engagements ship working systems in days, not months. Phased deliverables — every phase produces something working.
- **"Our team won't adopt it"** → We engineer for adoption. The CRM tells reps exactly who to call today. The receptionist replaces a missed-call log. The team uses it because it removes work, not adds it.
- **"How do I know this isn't just hype?"** → Two real systems you can call yourself: +1 (662) 670-1259 (Xovion's own receptionist — what your setup would feel like) and +1 (605) 937-5430 (Dump Dynasty — running in a real business taking real bookings). Production systems, not demos.
