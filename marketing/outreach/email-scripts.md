**Sequence:** First touch + 2 follow-ups (breakup on email 3)  
**Branch:** marketing-planning (never merges to main)

---

## Swap Tags

| Tag | Replace with |
|---|---|
| `[OWNER_NAME]` | Their first name |
| `[BUSINESS_NAME]` | Their business name |
| `[INDUSTRY]` | Their industry (HVAC company / dental practice / law firm / gym / plumbing business) |

## Email 1 — First Touch

**Send:** Day 1  
**Goal:** Get a reply or a click — not a sale

### Subject Line Options (pick one — test over time)

- `missed calls`
- `quick question about [BUSINESS_NAME]`
- `when no one picks up`

> [OWNER_NAME],
>
> Quick question — when someone calls [BUSINESS_NAME] and [INDUSTRY_PAIN], where does that call go?
>
> Most [INDUSTRY] owners I talk to have the same answer: voicemail. And most of those voicemails don't get returned fast enough, so the lead books whoever picks up first.
>
> We set up AI receptionists for [INDUSTRY] businesses that answer every call, qualify the lead, and book the appointment — automatically, 24/7. No extra staff. Flat monthly fee.
>
> Worth 15 minutes to see a live demo?
>
> Austin  
> Xovion Labs  
> [CALENDAR_LINK]

## Sending Notes

**Volume:** Start with 10–15 targeted sends per day — personalize [OWNER_NAME] and [BUSINESS_NAME] at minimum  
**Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am in their local timezone performs best for service business owners  
**Tool:** GoHighLevel sequences, Instantly, or manual with a CRM task reminder  
**Personalization floor:** At a minimum, every email should have their first name and business name filled in. Industry pain swap takes 30 seconds and meaningfully increases reply rate.  
**Track:** Log every send in campaign-tracker.csv with date, channel (email), and status

## Objection Handling (for replies)

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We already have a receptionist" | "Totally — this runs alongside them. The receptionist handles overflow: after-hours calls, busy signal situations, weekends. Most clients keep their staff and just stop losing leads when the desk isn't covered." |
| "How much does it cost?" | "Setup is $750 flat. After that it's $350/month. Most clients recover that in one or two appointments they would have missed. Happy to show you what the ROI looks like for [INDUSTRY] businesses specifically." |
| "We get the call to voicemail and call back" | "How quickly? Most service businesses call back within a few hours — but the lead has usually booked someone else by then. The receptionist answers in seconds and books on the spot." |
| "Send me more info" | Send the 2-minute demo video + calendar link. Don't send a PDF. |
