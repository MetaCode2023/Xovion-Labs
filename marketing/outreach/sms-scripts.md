# SMS Scripts — AI Receptionist Outreach

**Use case:** Warm leads and referrals only — not for cold lists  
**Offer:** AI Receptionist on GoHighLevel — $750 setup + $350/mo  
**Branch:** marketing-planning (never merges to main)

---

## When to Use SMS vs Email vs DM

| Channel | Best for |
|---|---|
| SMS | Warm leads, referrals, people you've met in person, follow-up after a call |
| Email | Cold outreach at scale, first touch to a business email |
| DM (LinkedIn/IG) | Cold outreach via social, after engaging with their content |

Do not send cold SMS to a list you scraped. It's legally and reputationally risky. Use SMS when there's already a reason to text them — a referral, a prior conversation, a networking event.

## SMS 1 — Referral Introduction

*(Use when someone specifically sent you their contact)*

> Hey [OWNER_NAME] — Austin from Xovion Labs. [REFERRAL_NAME] mentioned you might be a good fit for the AI receptionist we've been setting up for [INDUSTRY] businesses — answers every call, books appointments automatically, 24/7. Worth a quick look? [CALENDAR_LINK]

**Character count target:** Under 160 (single SMS). This one is ~175 — split send is fine, or trim [REFERRAL_NAME] mention if name is long.

---

## SMS 2 — Warm Lead (Met in Person / Prior Contact)

*(Use after a networking event, trade show, or referral intro where they've heard of you)*

> [OWNER_NAME] — Austin from Xovion Labs. Good meeting you at [EVENT/context]. Wanted to follow up on the AI receptionist — happy to send a 2-min demo video so you can see exactly how it works before we get on a call. Just reply "video" and I'll send it over.

---

## SMS 3 — Follow-Up After No Reply (Day 3)

*(Use if SMS 1 or 2 went unanswered — send once, then move to email)*

> [OWNER_NAME] — just making sure this didn't get buried. Happy to send a short video showing exactly how the receptionist works instead of a call. No pressure — just let me know.

---

## SMS 4 — After a Demo Call (Follow-Up)

*(Use within 24 hours of a demo where they didn't commit)*

> [OWNER_NAME] — great talking earlier. Here's the recap: setup is $750, then $350/month. I can have it live in under a week. If you want to move forward, just reply here or book the kickoff call: [CALENDAR_LINK]

---

## SMS Rules

- **Keep it under 160 characters when possible** — avoids split messages and looks cleaner
- **Don't pitch in the first SMS** — get a reply first, then pitch
- **One ask per text** — a video, a call, or a yes/no question. Never all three.
- **Reply within 5 minutes if they respond** — SMS carries a high expectation of speed
- **Max 2 follow-up texts** — if no response after 2, move them to email or mark as cold in the tracker
- **Log every send** in campaign-tracker.csv
