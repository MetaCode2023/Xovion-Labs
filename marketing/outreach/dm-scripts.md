## Notes on Tone

- Write like you're reaching out to a business contact, not cold-pitching a stranger
- Don't lead with "I'm Austin from Xovion Labs" — lead with their problem
- One ask per message: a demo call, not "let me know if you're interested"
- If they respond with any question at all, that's a warm lead — reply within the hour
