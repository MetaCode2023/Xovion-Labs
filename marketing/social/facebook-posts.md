## Post 4 — Objection Handling (Preemptive)

**Post to:** Business page — good for warming up an audience that's seen earlier posts

---

The two questions I always get when I show people the AI receptionist:

**"Will my customers know they're talking to AI?"**

Depends how you set it up. Some clients want it to be transparent — "Hi, this is the automated assistant for [Business Name]." Others want it to sound like a receptionist named Sarah. Both work. What matters is that the call gets answered and the appointment gets booked.

**"What if they have a complicated question?"**

The AI handles the booking and basic qualifying. For anything outside that scope, it takes a message and flags it for you, or transfers the call if you want. It's not trying to replace a conversation with you — it's making sure you're only having the important conversations, not playing phone tag.

These things are more robust than people expect. And simpler to get running than most software you already pay for.
