# TikTok Scripts — Xovion Labs

**5 video scripts ready to film**  
**Offer:** AI Receptionist — $750 setup + $350/mo  
**Format:** Hook (0–3s) → Build (3–30s) → Payoff + CTA (final 5–10s)  
**Target length:** 30–60 seconds each  
**Branch:** marketing-planning (never merges to main)

---

## Format Notes

- **Hook is everything.** If the first 2 seconds don't stop the scroll, the rest doesn't matter.
- Film in portrait (9:16). Good lighting. Clean background or relevant setting (office, field, etc.)
- Speak directly to camera — not reading, not scripted-sounding
- Use captions (auto-captions in TikTok or CapCut) — most people watch with sound off
- End every video with a single CTA: "Link in bio" or "DM me [WORD]"

## Script 1 — The Hook Play (Pattern Interrupt)

They leave a message — or they don't. And by the time you call back an hour later, they've already booked someone else."
